The Numbers That Drive
Real Revenue

Every metric in your campaign has a direct line to your bottom line. Here's what each one means for your business.

Open Rate

Visibility Turns into Opportunity

Open rates determine how many decision-makers actually see your message. A strong open rate means your subject lines cut through the noise and land in front of the right people — the first step to generating a lead.

Higher open rates = more eyes on your offer = more opportunities to start a conversation.

Reply Rate

Conversations That Convert

Reply rates reveal how many prospects are genuinely interested in what you offer. Each reply is a live sales conversation — a real human engaging with your pitch. This is where pipelines are built.

More replies = more sales conversations = more contracts signed.

Interest Rate

Qualified Leads, Not Just Noise

Interest rate measures the percentage of prospects who reply with genuine intent — asking for a quote, requesting a meeting, or wanting to learn more. These are your hottest leads, ready to buy.

High interest rates = a pipeline full of qualified buyers ready to close.
