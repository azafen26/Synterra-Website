The Numbers That Drive
Real Revenue

Every metric in your campaign has a direct line to your bottom line. Here's what each one means for your business.

Open Rate

Visibility Turns into Opportunity

Open rates determine how many decision-makers see your message. Strong subject lines cut through the noise and land in front of the right people — the first step to a lead.

Reply Rate

Conversations That Convert

Reply rates show how many prospects are genuinely interested. Each reply is a live sales conversation — a real human engaging with your pitch. This is where pipelines are built.

Interest Rate

Qualified Leads, Not Just Noise

Interest rate measures prospects who reply with genuine intent — asking for a quote, requesting a call, or wanting to learn more. These are your hottest, ready-to-buy leads.
